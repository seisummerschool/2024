Introduction

The International Summer School on Trustworthy Software is initiated in 2004 by Professor Jifeng He, academician of Chinese Academy of Sciences and founding dean of the School of Software Engineering of East China Normal University. The summer school aims to provide a platform for graduate students and young researchers in computer science or software engineering to learn state-of-the-art techniques in trustworthy software field. Every year, the summer school invites 3-5 international experts to offer excellent courses for the audients. It has been successfully held for 19 consecutive years in Shanghai, China.

The 19th summer school focuses on the topic "Formal Verification of Trustworthy AI Systems" and carries out a week-long activity in this summer.
